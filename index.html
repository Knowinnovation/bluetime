KiTimer

KiTimer is a collaborative timer, connecting through Bluetooth or WiFi. Setup one device as the display device and control it with the other.

Full Features List:

  • Connect 2 phones and share a timer
  • Apple Watch companion app
  • Full screen view for using device as a display
  • Automatically accept invitations from devices

KiTimer will soon be available on the App Store, download it for iPhone or iPad!
